Talon alphabet

This lesson is all about learning the Talon alphabet. On the right is a brief overview of all the different letters and their Talon counterparts. You can start the lesson by using go right and then enter or snap. You can stop the time by saying Escape enter. Don't worry if you get stuff wrong like your cursor bouncing around or wrong characters being placed. You will learn correcting these mistakes in later lessons.
If you wish to leave this lesson, you can press the 'Close' button, or simply say Control Whale to press Ctrl+W to close this tab.

Before you learn how to say words that make letters appear, we will go through how to activate the help menu for the alphabet. Saying help alphabet will make a context menu appear with all the letters and their linked words. If you want to close it again, you can say help close.
Below you will find the alphabet as it is presented by default in the knausj repository.

A Air B Bat C Cap D Drum E Each F Fine
G Gust H Harp I Sit J Jury K Krunch L Look
M Made N Near O Odd P Pit Q Quench R Red
S Sun T Trap U Urge V Vest W Whale X Plex
Y Yank Z Zip Ctrl Control Shift Shift Esc Escape spacebar Space

This is based on the knausj_talon repository. As with almost anything else in Talon, you can change the words for specific letters around if they do not recognize well. On top of that, if you change the words, they will show up in the help menu for the alphabet as well!
They can be changed in the file settings/alphabet.csv inside knausj_talon.
